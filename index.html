Case study 3

Unaware security guards


Security guards of some area appeared to be untrained and unaware when it comes to management of public space in their property.

Our reporter inquired about the public space at the reception of Hollywood Terrace. The guard replied by saying there was no public space on this property.

Later on, our reporter went to the public space via another route on Shing Wong Road and finally got it within falling on the radar of security.
